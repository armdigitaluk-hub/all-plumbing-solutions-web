    Frequently Asked Questions

    How much time should I leave between massage sessions?

    Ideally, you should have a session every 2-3 weeks to maintain the benefits. However, if you have injuries or severe stress, you can come weekly without a problem.

    Which type of massage is best for beginners?

    We recommend starting with a Swedish or Thai massage if you are looking for general relaxation. If you are looking for something more energetic, shiatsu is excellent for balancing.

    Do I need to arrive early to relax?

    Yes, we ask that you arrive 5-10 minutes early to complete a short form and allow your body to begin relaxing.

    Do the sessions include aromatic oils?

    All our massages include high-quality natural oils. If you have any allergies, please let us know when booking.

    Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

    You can cancel with 24 hours' notice at no charge. Within 24 hours, a 50% penalty is charged.

    Are the 5-session packages valid indefinitely?

    Packages are valid for 6 months from the date of purchase. Sessions are individual and non-transferable to other clients.
