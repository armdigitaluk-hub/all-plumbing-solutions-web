# Product Guidelines

## Design Philosophy
- **Aesthetic:** Calming, professional, clean.
- **Colors:** Earth tones (e.g., `#d4a574`, `#8b9d7e`), dark mode support (`#0d0f14` background).
- **Typography:** 'Syne' for headings (modern, humanist), 'JetBrains Mono' for technical/data elements.

## UX Principles
- **Clarity:** Clear labels and instructions.
- **Feedback:** Visual indicators for progress and completion.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast and keyboard navigability.
