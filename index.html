Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you charge a call-out fee just for turning up?

A: No. We do not charge a call-out fee. You only pay for the time spent working on your issue and any parts required. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden costs.

Q: How quickly can you attend an emergency?

A: We aim to be with you within 60-90 minutes for genuine emergencies, depending on your specific location and current traffic conditions. We are available 24/7.

Q: Are your engineers Gas Safe registered?

A: Yes, absolutely. All work involving gas boilers and appliances is carried out by fully qualified Gas Safe registered engineers. Safety is our top priority.

Q: How do I pay?

A: For most repair jobs, payment is due upon satisfactory completion of the work. We accept bank transfers, debit/credit cards, and cash.
