Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you charge a call-out or assessment fee?

A: No. Our quotes are always free with no hidden charges. You only pay once you are happy to proceed.

Q: Are your cleaners insured and vetted?

A: Yes, absolutely. Every team member is DBS-checked, reference-verified and covered by £2 million public liability insurance for your peace of mind.

Q: Do you supply your own equipment and products?

A: Yes. We bring all professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly, non-toxic products. Just let us know of any allergies or special surface requirements.

Q: How quickly can you book a clean?

A: In most cases we can arrange a clean within 24–48 hours. For urgent same-day requests, please call us directly and we will do our best to accommodate you.

Q: What is your satisfaction guarantee?

A: If you are not 100% satisfied with any area of your clean, contact us within 24 hours and we will return to re-clean that area at no extra charge — no questions asked.

Q: How do I pay?

A: Payment is due upon satisfactory completion. We accept bank transfer, all major debit/credit cards and cash. Recurring clients benefit from monthly invoicing.
